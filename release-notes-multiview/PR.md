Add NVENC encoding, exponential backoff, logo fixes, and keepalive (v0.2.1)

Restores NVIDIA NVENC hardware encoding as a selectable option in plugin settings, with CBR support and p1-p7 preset selection. Channel reconnects switch from a fixed 2s delay to exponential backoff capped at 60s, with a hard limit of 12 consecutive failures before a channel stops retrying. Logo rendering is fixed to preserve aspect ratio and handle transparency, logos load in the background instead of blocking startup, and HTTP URLs are now accepted in addition to local paths. A new keepalive mechanism registers a background client with Dispatcharr's live_proxy to keep channels warm between viewer connections. Also fixes file descriptor leaks on shutdown and corrects a 200-with-empty-body response when a channel is not yet ready.

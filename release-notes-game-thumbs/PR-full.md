Release v2.0.0: athlete support, new styles, event overlays, major league expansion

Adds athlete thumbnails for tennis, MMA, and boxing via ESPN and TheSportsDB providers; badge overlays on all route types; styles 5/6 (grid), 98 (3D embossed), and a redesigned style 1; `winner=` greyscale effect; generic event overlay parameters (`title=`, `subtitle=`, `iconurl=`) behind `ALLOW_EVENT_OVERLAYS`; custom font registry with disk-backed caching; custom team support via `teams.json`; and a large league expansion covering 10 rugby leagues, KBO, all MiLB levels, CBL, IIHF, Unrivaled Basketball, US Open Cup, and USL Championship. Also renames two rugby league keys (`epr` to `prem`, `ercc` to `epcr`), switches AHL to HockeyTech, fixes WHL/OHL/QMJHL team resolution, bundles 11 league logos locally to eliminate Wikimedia 429 errors, adds opt-in SOCKS5 proxy support for HockeyTech, and removes the `/xcproxy` route.
